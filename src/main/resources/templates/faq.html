What are some insights into the strategy or vision at MCPP?

Pros

The founders are constantly on the hunt for ideas to improve the platform and are always open to suggestions from the team (be it an intern or a full timer’s opinion). Interns are given as much responsibility as a full timer and are constantly given feedback. Everyone is super friendly and always up for an outing/team event after work

Cons

Some things change rapidly and can be hard to keep up with, but it is expected in any growing startup industry

What is the company culture at MCPP?

Pros

- the founder are trustworthy, very smart, professional and have created a wonderful balanced culture between work hard and let's have fun - the team is one of a kind and it's for me the first time to say "I wanna work with ALL of them again!" - the businessmodel is future oriented, very thought through and customer oriented developed - the environment in general has the right dynamic of a start up with the right professionalism from corporates

Cons

- honestly, only the stairs towards the office

What are co-workers like at MCPP?

Pros

I had a brilliant time at Datarade! There was constantly a friendly and energetic atmosphere in the office and we also had lots of fun social events. It was inspiring to be part of such a close-knit team made up of interesting, talented, and driven people and work together with them towards a meaningful and exciting mission. Datarade let me gain responsibility and take ownership of projects, which I really appreciated! Everyone was very supportive and the work culture promoted creativity and openness to new ideas, so I always felt that my opinions were listened to and valued.

Cons

No cons – I genuinely had a great experience!
